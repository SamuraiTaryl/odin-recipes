BBQ Pulled Pork and Cheddar Loaded Mashed Potatoes

Description:

HANDS DOWN THE MOST INSANELY DELICIOUS MASHED POTATOES CONCOCTION EVER CONCEIVED. PERIOD.

  • Ingredients Needed:
  • 5 pounds of yellow or red potatoes
  • 1 package of Jack Daniels pulled pork (the microwable package you find at grocery stores)
  • 1 stick of butter (1/4 pound of butter; or you can go with 2 sticks if your fiend)
  • 1 quart of cream (or half-and-half or whole milk)
  • 1 pound of shredded sharp cheddar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Boil the potatoes in a large stock pot over high heat until the skins are starting to come apart.
    2. Drain the water and leave the potatoes in the stock pot (take extra precaution to avoid scalding hot water).
    3. Mash in the cream and butter, and then salt and pepper to taste as if you were making the mashed potatoes with these ingredients alone.
    4. Add the the pulled pork and cheddar and mash these into the mix.
    5. Put the mashed potatoes back onto the range over medium-low heat and stir occasionally to ensure uniform heating of all ingredients.
    6. Serve to your family and/or friends and become their culinary hero!
