Buiscuits and Gravy

Description:

Where comfort food and breakfast intersect is where you will find buiscuits and gravy.

  • Ingredients Needed:
  • 1 package - Pillsbury Flaky buiscuits
  • 1 pound mild breakfast sausage
  • 1/3 gallon whole milk
  • 5 tablespoons whole flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt

    • Steps

    1. Cook the buiscuits according to the instructions on the container
    2. While the oven is pre-heating grab a large frying pan and cook the sausage over medium heat.
    3. Dont forget to pop the buiscuits in the oven.
    4. Once the sausage is cooked to a uniform brown DO NOT DRAIN THE FATS!
    5. At this point add the butter and the flour to make the rue for the gravy. This is what will make the gravy nice and thick!
    6. After cooking the rue for two or three minutes it is now time to add the milk, salt and pepper.
    7. Continue cooking over medium heat and stirring every so often.
    8. Make sure to check on those buiscuits!
    9. Once the gravy begins boiling turn the heat down to low and keep stiring every so oftern, paying particularly close attention to scraping the bottom and the sides of the pan.
    10. Once the gravy is no longer boiling you will want to turn the heat back up to medium, staying on top of the stirring/scraping.
    11. Keep alternating the heat from medium to low and back to medium through several boiling cycles. This will get the gravy nice and thick!
    12. Now just plop that deliciousness on a plate and plan for the imminent food coma that comes afterwards.
