Candied Oranges

Description:

Turning nature's candy into actual candy? YES PLEASE!

  • Ingredients Needed:
  • Oranges
  • Sugar
  • Water

    • Steps

    1. Cut your oranges into slices approximately 3/16" to 1/4" thick.
    2. Put equal parts sugar and water into a frying pan (1 or 2 cups of each (or more if performing this task in a larger pan)).
    3. Turn the burner onto medium heat (hot enough to boil lightly).
    4. Add the orange slices.
    5. Boil the oranges and water/sugar mixture down into a simple syrup.
    6. Boil until the orange slices are translucent (the longer you go the harder the end product will be).
